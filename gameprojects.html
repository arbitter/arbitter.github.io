Game Projects

Listed on this page are all the game projects I have worked on, the role(s) I performed on each project, and any media I have for each project.



Polarity

Polarity was a group project I worked on as an undergraduate. Polarity is a 2D platformer where the character makes use of magnets to reach the end of the level.


Polarity was programmed in C++ with DirectX11 and an in-house engine our professor provided to us.

On polarity, I worked as a Level Designer, AI Programmer, Engine Programmer, Gameplay Programmer, Input Programmer, and Generalist Programmer.


Asunder

Asunder was an individual project I worked on as an undergraduate. Asunder is a 3D RPG where the player alternates between two differnt characters as they play.



Asunder was programmed in C++ with Unreal Engine 4 and Unreal Motion Graphics.

Since Asunder was a game that I developed on my own, I took on all programming roles for this project.
